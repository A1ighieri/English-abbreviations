About our project

Nowadays many people communicate through messages on the Internet, and in order to simplify the set of messages, we use various abbreviations from time to time. Having met them in Russian, we can easily guess the meaning of abbreviated words, but in English this task becomes much more difficult. And, since we can often find English-language abbreviations and abbreviations in movies when we are shown a phone screen, games and when communicating with foreigners, we decided to analyze the use of abbreviations in online correspondence in English and determine their main types. On this website you can see the results of our research.

However, we would not like to dwell on the results obtained. By clicking on the button below, you can share with us what other abbreviations you have met and we will definitely add them to our list. Thank you for taking the time to our project.
